BondMakers Home Loans

BondMakers Home Loans is an innovative WordPress-based website offering a suite of tools for home loan applicants. Features include:

  • Interactive calculators for bond qualifying and monthly repayments.
  • A comprehensive prequalification form.
  • Downloadable editable PDF application forms.
  • A detailed contact page for user inquiries.
  • Responsive design for optimal user experience across various devices.
  • WordPress configuration and theme customization tailored to client needs.
  • Integration of dynamic calculators and forms to enhance user interaction.
  • Effective content management enabling easy updates and maintenance.

My role encompassed the entire website development process, ensuring that each feature was implemented to meet both functional and aesthetic requirements.
